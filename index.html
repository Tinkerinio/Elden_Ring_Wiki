When did Elden Ring release?

Elden Ring was released for all platforms on February 25th of 2022. The original release date of
January 21st was announced during the Summer Game Fest 2021, and the delay was revealed via Twitter
on October 18th 2021

Is there Elden Ring DLC?

Shadow of the Erdtree is the only DLC and expansion for Elden Ring. Announced via twitter by the
official Elden Ring account, it was launched on June 21st 2024 and added a large amount of content.

What Platforms is Elden Ring On?

Elden Ring is available for PS4, Xbox One, PC. Elden Ring is available for Playstation 5 and Xbox One X as a FREE upgrade.
